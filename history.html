Important Peoples

Ma. Ella F. Fabella

A Master Teacher II at Maasin Learning Center in Zamboanga City, recognized as one of the 2024 Metrobank Foundation Outstanding Filipinos for her dedication to inclusive education and innovative teaching programs. She's a passionate advocate for Sama-Bajau children, encouraging them to take pride in their heritage through initiatives like "Project B.E.A.R." and the "Pantawid Tutorial Reading Program".

Franco Rino C. Apoyon

A Head Teacher II at Kabasalan National High School in Zamboanga Sibugay, honored as one of the 2024 Metrobank Foundation Outstanding Filipinos for his remarkable contributions to basic education. He's an environmental education advocate who promotes "Greenducation" through the Youth for Environment in Schools Program and created an award-winning environmental storybook and sustainable products.
